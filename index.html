Security Tips

What makes a password strong?

Length matters most. A 20+ character password is harder to brute force than a short complex one.

Mixing uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols increases the search space and raises entropy.

Use unique passwords for every account, especially email, banking, and admin access.
